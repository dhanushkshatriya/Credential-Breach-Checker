Zero-Knowledge System (How you stay safe)

When you type your sensitive data (like a password) into Credential Breach Checker, it never leaves your computer in plain text.

01. Scrambling Text Locally

Your browser uses secure math to turn your password into a scrambled puzzle that can never be reversed.

02. Sending Safe Clues

For passwords, we split the scrambled puzzle in two. We only send the first tiny piece (5 letters) to our server. The server replies with a list of matches, without ever knowing your actual password.

03. Private Checking

Your computer compares the secret remaining piece to the server's list. The server never learns which specific password you checked.
